If you are appyling for your first passport, you must apply at an acceptance facility unless it is a life-or-death emergency (please make an appointment at a passport agency). The cost of your passport, paid to the U.S. Department of State, must be a check or money order. There is a $35 processing fee per person at acceptance facilities. The $35 processing fee can be paid in a number of ways. Check with your acceptance facility before arriving to see what forms of payment they accept.

1-2 day return delivery is not available for passport cards alone.
