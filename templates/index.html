Hello, world!

With a passport, we can all say "Hello, world."

A passport allows you to travel the world and experience new cultures. But before you can travel with your passport, you have to go through the experience of actually getting a passport.

Since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic, the U.S. Department of State has been bogged down with the demand for passports, by having too many applications to process and having too many phone calls to answer. The U.S. mail system has also suffered greatly during the pandemic. Due to both of these new strains on the U.S. Department of State and the U.S. Postal Service, it has taken longer than usual to receive your passport and the documents you submit along with your application.

This site has been created to help passport applicants through the process, as a more personal supplement to the U.S. Department of State website. Here, you have access to an interactive checklist of materials to bring, a cost calculator, and an estimated time of arrival for your passport book or card.

Enjoy!
